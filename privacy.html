Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Overview

Tooth Extraction: Recovery Log ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Tooth Extraction: Recovery Log does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Everything you enter (your extraction details, dates, symptom logs, notes, and notification settings) is stored locally on your device only. The app works fully offline.

What We Don't Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No location data
  • No health or medical records
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers

Local Storage

The app stores your extraction sites, recovery timeline inputs, symptom logs, and notification preferences locally on your device using Hive (a local database). This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all stored data.

No Network Access

All recovery timelines are computed on your device from the date and time you enter. The app does not require an internet connection and does not send any information to any server.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Push Notifications

The app uses local notifications only (scheduled on your device) for optional saltwater-rinse and care reminders. No data is sent to any push notification server. You can enable or disable notifications in the app's settings.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Used for subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data as described in their privacy policy. No personally identifiable information is shared.

Not Medical Advice

This app is an informational timing tool. It does not diagnose dry socket, infection, or any complication, and it does not replace the instructions of your dentist or oral surgeon. Always follow the aftercare guidance you were given and contact your dental provider with any concern.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
